We have great news for developers interested in understanding the technology behind Bitcoin! We are glad to announce the launch of the Blockchain Innovation Programme, a 10-week learning programme to help you understand the fundamentals of blockchain technology.

The programme, which will be first held in Uganda, East Africa, will help aspiring tech entrepreneurs and leaders like you build the confidence to pursue a career in the blockchain industry by offering you a mix of online courses, coaching sessions and meetups, where you can:

  • Master the basic functionalities of the BSV blockchain (BSV)
  • Acquire practical skills to start successfully building basic applications on BSV
  • Familiarise yourself with programming languages used for BSV projects (GoLang, Like JS)

Officially launching on September 2 at MOTIV, the Blockchain Innovation Programme will start accepting registrations until September 19, 2022 with an application fee of only $50, refundable to unsuccessful applicants.
