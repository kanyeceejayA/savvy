Our SME Bridge Fund

Together with our investors, we are committed to accelerating growth of our economy through facilitating financing solutions to Small and Medium Enterprises.

Our first fund, The SME Bridge Fund, is a credit fund aimed at providing innovative short-term debt financing solutions to SMEs such as; receivables financing, import tax financing, supplier top-ups and stock financing, payment of freight and taxes, contract/LPO financing, commodity financing and bid bond guarantee financing to mention but a few.

Investors

Our anchor investors are institutional investors, including foundations, pension funds, family offices and high net worth individuals.

Reginald Tumisiime
Co-Founder & Director

Reginald has over 8 years’ experience in banking and finance at leading financial institutions. Reginald is responsible for daily operations of the firm as well as business development. He has very strong interpersonal skills that have enabled him to develop and grow strategic relationships within both the private sector and public sector. His analytical mind is also invaluable to the firm.

He was most recently the Manager Capital, Stanbic Bank – the largest local bank by Balance sheet and revenue. Reginald started his career at Standard Chartered Bank, where he joined the bank through the prestigious international graduate program in 2012. He rose through the ranks to serve as the Regional Head Business Clients Leads Management, for the Standard Chartered Bank Africa Regional Office, where he was in charge of big data analytics solutions for the Business Banking Client Segment across the 8 markets of Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Ghana, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana and Nigeria.

Prior to that, he worked in several functions within StanChart Uganda such as Finance where he took on different transformational projects, Retail Segments where he was the Segment Proposition Head for the retail premium segment, Priority Banking where Wealth Management was the key pillar.

Reginald holds a BSc in Statistics (Hons), and is currently pursuing the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) program.
