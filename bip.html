Blockchain Innovation Program

Capitalsavvy has partnered with the BSV Blockchain Association to launch the Blockchain Innovation Program in East Africa.

The Blockchain Innovation Program is aimed at equipping young entrepreneurs with relevant web 3 developer skills. It is 10-week program, with a robust curriculum aimed at providing the relevant foundation required for developers to build scalable projects and enterprises on the BSV Blockchain, a proof of work Blockchain. The first cohort will have a total of 50 successful entrepreneurs, that will be carefully selected based on several surrogates including a demonstration of a strong understanding of web 2 developer skills, among others. We plan to have a minimum of three cohorts a year.

Incorporated in the program, is a hackathon at the end of the program, aimed at providing an opportunity for the best evaluated entrepreneur to represent the rest of the cohort at the Block Dojo, in London. This will provide an avenue for further learning, and linkages to potential seed/ venture capital partners for the entrepreneur. The rest of the entrepreneurs in the program will be exposed to several projects within the BSV Blockchain ecosystem where they will have an opportunity to be gainfully employed, thereby applying the acquired skills.
